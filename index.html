What it does

Forged for the fight

A complete offline assistant that runs your battles, simulates them without spending resources, and optimizes what you take in.

Marquee feature

Key-free Clan Boss simulation

PyAutoRaid runs the game's real battle engine to play out your Clan Boss fight offline — turn by turn, hit by hit — without spending a single key.

Test a tune, a re-gear, or a new champion and watch exactly how the fight unfolds before it costs you anything.

cb_sim.py — void UNM
$ pyautoraid cb sim ────────────────────────── turn 12 Maneater A1 turn 13 Demytha A3 [Block DMG] turn 14 Ninja A2 [detonate] ... survives → T50 enrage total dmg 36.4M ✓ keys spent: 0

Team & gear optimizer

Assign artifacts to hit location-specific stat targets — ACC floors, speed tunes, crit and damage caps — and get a clean diff of only the swaps that improve your team.

Battle history

Every fight is logged in full — per-turn turn meter, buffs, debuffs, per-hit damage. Review any run, compare tunes over time, and calibrate against reality.

Automation — optional

Run a location N times, or schedule daily runs, entirely headless. Every automated action goes through the mod API — never screen automation. Fully opt-in.

Runs entirely on your PC

PyAutoRaid runs locally on your Windows machine alongside the game. No cloud, no account sharing — your data stays with you.

How it works

Trustworthy by design

Because PyAutoRaid drives the game's own battle engine, what you see in the sim is what you'll get in the fight.

1

A local mod, attached to your game

A lightweight BepInEx mod loads with Raid on your PC and exposes a small local API. Nothing runs remotely.

2

Mod API only — never screen automation

Every action reads and writes through the game's real objects. No pixel-hunting, no fake clicks, no bots watching your screen.

3

The real engine powers the sim

Simulations run the same battle processor the live game uses — so turn order, procs, and damage match what actually happens.

4

Your art, extracted from your game

Champion and item art is read from your own installed game on first run. Nothing proprietary is bundled or redistributed.

The engine is the game's. The sim isn't a guess — it's the fight.

That's what makes "before you spend a key" a promise, not a slogan.
