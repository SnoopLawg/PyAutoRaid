# Double Counter-Attack

**Type**: Traditional · **Difficulty**: Easy · **Key**: 2 Key UNM · **Affinity**: All Affinities

Source: https://deadwoodjedi.com/speed-tunes/double-counter-attack/

Using 2 Counter-Attack Champions to stay survive longer and maximize damage.

## Slot Rules

### Slot 1 · Counter-Attack 1

Should be last in turn order

### Slot 2 · Counter-Attacker 2

Should typically be 3rd in turn order (depending on the champs used can go before your stun target)

### Slot 3 · Stun Target Tank

Typically fits between the Counter-Attackers in turn order to ensure buffs fall off to guide the stun to them.

## Notes

Using 2 Counter-Attack Champions to stay survive longer and maximize damage.



ALL SPEEDS LISTED ARE TRUE SPEEDS.
Speeds listed exclude any speed aura.  Speed Auras can be used to lower the BUILD SPEEDS.
Use the calculator to check speeds and take into account any Area Bonuses your account may have.
Make sure to prioritize all the skills in the Team Setup.

Speed Ranges:


As with nearly every Speed Tune, there are more speeds that will work than are listed, but these are the ones tested that work best and most consistently for the approved difficulties and affinities.  Generally changing the speeds of one champion can change the speeds needed of other champions.  Feel free to play around in the calculator to find what works best for your team.  There are wider ranges and alternative speeds for just one difficulty. 





All champs going before Clan Boss: 191-209
All champs going after Clan Boss: 171-189



Speed Tune Sync:



UNM tune will sync on turn 1
NM tune will sync on turn 1

Skill Set Up:



UNM

Slow CA: Open CA Skill
Fast CA: Open A1, then CA Skill



Gear, Masteries, Blessings:



Gear

Any sets that don’t affect turn meter or skill timing for the key players (Counter Attack Champs).
No relentless.


Masteries

Stick to typical Masteries that won’t affect Turn Meter or skill timing.


Blessings

Stick to typical Clan Boss Blessings.  ( 1 Brimstone, 1 Cruelty, Remainder Phantom Touch )



Advanced Notes:



These two ranges work with each other for UNM and can work for Nightmare, but some adjustment maybe necessary.​ CHECK IN THE CALCULATOR!
When mixing these speeds, they are roughly equivalent to the speed with a 20 points difference. Between the two the higher one will go first. So 191 will go before 171, but both will go after 172 and higher.
When using Skullcrusher make the other Counter-Attack Champion last in the turn order.  That CA champ should use their ability before the AOE2.
Skullcrusher can be placed in either CA position – BUT when using Skullcrusher as the second CA champion, and to ensure the stun goes to a champion other than your CA champs, you must have the tank slower than Skullcrusher. They also must be in a higher team order than the CA champions. This is to ensure the buff falls off that champion before the stun, forcing him to be targeted.
