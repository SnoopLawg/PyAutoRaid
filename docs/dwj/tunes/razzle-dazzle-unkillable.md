# Razzle Dazzle Unkillable

**Type**: Unkillable · **Difficulty**: Moderate · **Key**: 1 Key UNM · **Affinity**: All Affinities · **Author**: DeadwoodJedi and ShortonSkillz

Source: https://deadwoodjedi.com/speed-tunes/razzle-dazzle-unkillable/

Unkillable Clan Boss team focused around the crazy speeds of Razelvarg to incredibly fast and do great damage!

## Slot Rules

### Slot 1 · Razelvarg

Razelvarg opens a2, then a3.  This can be prioritized in the team setup.


You CANNOT use Counter-Attack on Razelvarg.  Ally-Attack’s must be used immediately before Razelvarg takes his turn.


Razelvarg Specific:  No Masteries or Gear that can grant Counter-Attack, Turn Meter, Buff Extension, or Skill Reduction.

### Slot 2 · Unkillable/Block Damage Champion

Ideal slot for Protection (Ally-Protection, Increase Def, Strengthen, etc) Champion.

### Slot 3 · Unkillable/Block Damage Champion

Ideal slot for Protection (Ally-Protection, Increase Def, Strengthen, etc) Champion.

### Slot 5 · Cleanser/DPS

The first stun will not be cleansed, so plan your skills accordingly.

## Notes

Works for both UNM and NM.  When utilizing Razelvargs speed aura this is arguably the slowest 2:1 ratio teams in the game.



ALL SPEEDS LISTED ARE TRUE SPEEDS.
Speeds listed exclude any speed aura. If you use an aura you should reduce your build speed accordingly.
Use the calculator to find the build speed and take into account any Area Bonuses your account may have.
True speeds do not require you to add bonus speed from Razelvarg passive. The build speeds factor in his passive and is calculated automatically by the calculator.

 


Speed Ranges:


As with nearly every Speed Tune, there are more speeds that will work than are listed, but these are the ones tested that work best and most consistently for the approved difficulties and affinities.  Generally changing the speeds of one champion can change the speeds needed of other champions.  Feel free to play around in the calculator to find what works best for your team.

250.55-252 Unkillable/Block damage
250.55-252 Unkillable/Block damage
231-250 DPS
187-190 Cleanser/DPS

There are situations where hitting the low end of Razz’s speed will create a conflict with the high end of the other champs, so I’d keep the speed 264 and above.

Spirit affinity on UNM can be problematic if Razz resists the first Decrease Speed debuff.  If this happens, Razz at 264 speed and faster will keep things in sync, but the turn order of the DPS and slowest Protection Champs will be inverted.


Speed Tune Sync:



UNM tune will sync on turn 4
NM tune will sync on turn 6
Brutal tune will sync on turn 6 (turn 7 on Spirit Affinity)

Spirit affinity on UNM can be problematic if Razz resists the first Decrease Speed debuff.  If this happens, Razz at 264 speed and faster will keep things in sync, but the turn order of the DPS and slowest Protection Champs will be inverted.


Spirit affinity on UNM will also necessitate a slight alteration to the skill timing of the slower Protection Champ to maintain proper skill order.


Skill Set Up:



Razelvarg opens a2, then a3.  This can be prioritized in the team setup.
The fastest of the Unkillable Champs will go first in the turn order.  Unkillable or Block Damage both work.

Gear, Masteries, Blessings:



Gear

Any sets that don’t affect turn meter or skill timing for the key players (Razz, both Unkillable/Block Damage Champs, Cleanser).  No relentless.

Razelvarg Specific:  No Gear that can grant Counter-Attack, Turn Meter, Buff Extension, or Skill Reduction.


Masteries

Stick to typical Masteries that won’t affect Turn Meter or skill timing.
Razelvarg Specific:  No Masteries that can grant Counter-Attack, Turn Meter, Buff Extension, or Skill Reduction.


Blessings

Stick to typical Clan Boss Blessings.



Advanced Notes:



Spirit affinity on UNM can be problematic if Razz resists the first Decrease Speed debuff.  If this happens, Razz at 264 speed and faster will keep things in sync, but the turn order of the DPS and slowest Protection Champs will be inverted.
Spirit affinity on UNM will also necessitate a slight alteration to the skill timing of the slower Unkillable/Block Damage Champ to maintain proper skill order.
The stun will NOT be cleansed on UNM (along with affinity debuffs depending on the difficulty) until fully in sync, so plan your skills accordingly.
You CANNOT use Counter-Attack on Razelvarg.  Ally-Attack’s must be used immediately before Razelvarg takes his turn.
Razelvarg Specific:  No Masteries or Gear that can grant Counter-Attack, Turn Meter, Buff Extension, or Skill Reduction.
