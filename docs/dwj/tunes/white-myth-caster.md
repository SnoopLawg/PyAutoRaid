# White-Myth-Caster

**Type**: Unkillable · **Difficulty**: Moderate · **Key**: 2 Key UNM · **Affinity**: All Affinities · **Author**: DeadwoodJedi

Source: https://deadwoodjedi.com/speed-tunes/white-myth-caster/

Unkillable comp abusing Demytha's 3 turn cooldown block damage to stay unkillable!

## Notes

Ultra Nightmare ONLY!!!



ALL SPEEDS LISTED ARE TRUE SPEEDS.
Speeds listed exclude any speed aura.  Speed Auras can be used to lower the BUILD SPEEDS.
Use the calculator to check speeds and take into account any Area Bonuses your account may have.
Make sure to prioritize all the skills in the Team Setup.

Speed Ranges:


As with nearly every Speed Tune, there are more speeds that will work than are listed, but these are the ones tested that work best and most consistently for the approved difficulties and affinities.  Generally changing the speeds of one champion can change the speeds needed of other champions.  Feel free to play around in the calculator to find what works best for your team.





256-259 – Warcaster
254-255 – Demytha

Must be Slower than Warcaster


205-211 – DPS
195-204 – DPS / Block Debuff
183-189 – Nia



Speed Tune Sync:



UNM tune will sync on turn 4

Skill Set Up:



UNM

Warcaster: Open A1, then A2, then A3
Demytha: Open A3, then A2
Block Debuff: Open A1, then Block Debuff Skill
Nia: Open A1, then A3, then A2



Gear, Masteries, Blessings:



Gear

Any sets that don’t affect turn meter or skill timing for the key players (Demytha/Block Debuff/Warcaster/Nia).
Relentless is okay on DPS that DO NOT have Turn Meter effecting skills.


Masteries

Stick to typical Masteries that won’t affect Turn Meter or skill timing.


Blessings

Stick to typical Clan Boss Blessings ( 1 Brimstone, 1 Cruelty, Remainder Phantom Touch )



Advanced Notes:



Demytha and Nia have NO replacements.
Demytha must be in the lead taking the stuns.
On Affinity you must use the same affinity or weak affinity DPS.
Against Spirit Affinity you need a Block Debuffs champ with a 3 turn Cooldown.
Ninja works, at 193-195 speed without Cycle of Magic or Counterattack masteries / accessories.
