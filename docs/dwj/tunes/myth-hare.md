# Myth Hare

**Type**: Unkillable · **Difficulty**: Hard · **Key**: 1 Key UNM · **Affinity**: All Affinities · **Author**: Sir Henry White

Source: https://deadwoodjedi.com/speed-tunes/myth-hare/

Unkillable Clan Boss team focused around the crazy speeds of Razelvarg and Demytha to go incredibly fast and do great damage!

## Slot Rules

### Slot 1 · Razelvarg

Razelvarg opens a3, then a2.  This can be prioritized in the team setup. For spirit you will open a1, a1, a2, a3


You CANNOT use Counter-Attack on Razelvarg.  Ally-Attack’s must be used immediately before Razelvarg takes his turn.


Razelvarg Specific:  No Masteries or Gear that can grant Counter-Attack, Turn Meter, Buff Extension, or Skill Reduction.

### Slot 2 · Demytha

Ideal slot for Protection (Ally-Protection, Increase Def, Strengthen, etc) Champion.

### Slot 3 · Block Debuff

Must have Block Debuff for 2 turn duration on 3 turn cooldown.


Must be faster than Seeker

Must be Slower than Razelvarg

### Slot 4 · Seeker

Open a1


Must be slower than Block Debuff Champion

### Slot 5 · DPS

Exceedingly tight speed range

## Notes

Works for both UNM, NM and Brutal.



ALL SPEEDS LISTED ARE TRUE SPEEDS.
Speeds listed exclude any speed aura. If you use an aura you should reduce your build speed accordingly.
Use the calculator to find the build speed and take into account any Area Bonuses your account may have.
True speeds do not require you to add bonus speed from Razelvarg passive. The build speeds factor in his passive and is calculated automatically by the calculator.

 


Speed Ranges:


As with nearly every Speed Tune, there are more speeds that will work than are listed, but these are the ones tested that work best and most consistently for the approved difficulties and affinities.  Generally changing the speeds of one champion can change the speeds needed of other champions.  Feel free to play around in the calculator to find what works best for your team. Razelvarg is particularly difficult to program into the calculator, it is possible you will see some slight variances depending on the difficulties from the calculator to in game results.  We are working on it, but be forewarned.



315-316.75 Demytha
210-240 Razelvarg
209-239 Block Debuff
208.36-210 Seeker
194.91-195.59 DPS

The Block Debuff Champions speed must be faster than Seeker and slower than Razelvarg. Spirit ranges for DPS are tighter than non-spirit.  Jintoro will have a wider range as well (or at least different), due to his passive. Not all ranges have been tested, so be careful using speeds near the recommended edges.


Speed Tune Sync:



UNM tune will sync on turn 2
NM tune will sync on turn 2
Brutal tune will sync on turn 2

Spirit must be manualled on turn zero (double a1 for Demytha, then a2)


Skill Set Up:



Skill set up is the same for UNM, NM and Brutal
Demytha opens a3, then a2.  This can be prioritized in the team setup.
For Spirit Demytha will need to a1 twice on turn zero and then a2, then a3 (prioritize these)
Block Damage Champ has 1 turn delay for debuff block.
Seeker opens with a1

Gear, Masteries, Blessings:



Gear

Any sets that don’t affect turn meter or skill timing for the key players (Razz, both Unkillable/Block Damage Champs, Cleanser).  No relentless.

Razelvarg Specific:  No Gear that can grant Counter-Attack, Turn Meter, Buff Extension, or Skill Reduction.


Masteries

Stick to typical Masteries that won’t affect Turn Meter or skill timing.
Razelvarg Specific:  No Masteries that can grant Counter-Attack, Turn Meter, Buff Extension, or Skill Reduction.


Blessings

Stick to typical Clan Boss Blessings.



Advanced Notes:



For Spirit Demytha will need to a1 twice on turn zero and then a2, then a3 (prioritize these)
Ninja (or other champs that grant themselves turn meter) will NOT work
You CANNOT use Counter-Attack on Razelvarg.  Ally-Attack’s must be used immediately before Razelvarg takes his turn.
Razelvarg Specific:  No Masteries or Gear that can grant Counter-Attack, Turn Meter, Buff Extension, or Skill Reduction.
