## Notes

There are multiple variations of this style team simply by altering the speeds slightly.  The ranges on this particular tune are very tight, so make sure to double check (both on affinity and off).



ALL SPEEDS LISTED ARE TRUE SPEEDS.
Speeds listed exclude any speed aura.  Speed Auras can be used to lower the BUILD SPEEDS.
Use the calculator to check speeds and take into account any Area Bonuses your account may have.
Make sure to prioritize all the skills in the Team Setup.

Speed Ranges:


As with nearly every Speed Tune, there are more speeds that will work than are listed, but these are the ones tested that work best and most consistently for the approved difficulties and affinities.  Generally changing the speeds of one champion can change the speeds needed of other champions.  Feel free to play around in the calculator to find what works best for your team.  There are wider ranges and alternative speeds for just one difficulty. 





245-246 – Sepulcher Sentinel
239-240 – 4:3 DPS
187-189 – DPS
185 – DPS
171-172 – Valkyrie



Speed Tune Sync:



UNM tune will sync on turn 3
NM tune will sync on turn 3

Skill Set Up:



UNM

Sepulcher Sentinel: Open with A1, then A2
Valkyrie: Open with A1, then A1, then A2




NM

Sepulcher Sentinel: Open with A1, then A1, then A2
Valkyrie: Open with A2



Gear, Masteries, Blessings:



Gear

Any sets that don’t affect turn meter or skill timing for the key players (Valkyrie/Sepulcher Sentinel).  No relentless.


Masteries

Stick to typical Masteries that won’t affect Turn Meter or skill timing.


Blessings

Stick to typical Clan Boss Blessings.  ( 1 Brimstone, 1 Cruelty, Remainder Phantom Touch )



Advanced Notes:



UNM

4:3 DPS champion will take a double turn after the stun and before Valkyrie on void and before the stun on affinity.
The 185 champion will go after valkyrie on affinity.
You’ll want to ensure everyone has increase defense before the stun or the other 4:3 champion will take the stun on affinity when the increase defense buff from Sepulcher Sentinel falls off.


NM

The 4:3 DPS champion will take a double turn between the aoe’s.
You’ll want to ensure everyone has increase defense before the stun or the other 4:3 champion will take the stun on affinity when the increase defense buff from Sepulcher Sentinel falls off.


It will be near impossible to keep increase defense up for the full fight, but you should be able to still get great results and last a LONG time with this setup.
