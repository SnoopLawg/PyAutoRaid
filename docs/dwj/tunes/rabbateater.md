# RabBatEater

**Type**: Unkillable · **Difficulty**: Moderate · **Key**: 1 Key UNM · **Affinity**: All Affinities · **Author**: ShortonSkillz

Source: https://deadwoodjedi.com/speed-tunes/rabbateater/

Unkillable Clan Boss team focused around the crazy speeds of Razelvarg and two Block Damage or Unkillable Champions.

## Slot Rules

### Slot 1 · Razelvarg

You CANNOT use Counter-Attack on Razelvarg.  Ally-Attack’s must be used immediately before Razelvarg takes his turn.


Razelvarg Specific:  No Masteries or Gear that can grant Counter-Attack, Turn Meter, Buff Extension, or Skill Reduction.

### Slot 3 · Maneater (Slow)

One Unkillable/Block Damage Champ will have a 1 turn delay on their ability (a1, a3 for example) and the other a 3 turn delay (a1, a1, a1, a3 for example).  It does not matter which.  This is true for all difficulties.


The fastest of the Unkillable/Block Damage Champs will go first in the turn order.  This means if you wish you use Warcaster he must be the slower of the two.

## Notes

Works for both UNM and NM.  When utilizing Razelvargs speed aura this is arguably the slowest 2:1 ratio teams in the game.



ALL SPEEDS LISTED ARE TRUE SPEEDS.
Speeds listed exclude any speed aura. If you use an aura you should reduce your build speed accordingly.
Use the calculator to find the build speed and take into account any Area Bonuses your account may have.
True speeds do not require you to add bonus speed from Razelvarg passive. The build speeds factor in his passive and is calculated automatically by the calculator.

Speed Tune Sync:



UNM tune will sync on turn 5

Brutal tune will sync on turn 3

Skill Set Up:



UNM and NM:

Razelvarg opens a3, then a2.  This can be prioritized in the team setup.
Maneater (Fast) has no delays, opening with their a3 skill.
Maneater (Slow) has a 2 turn delay (a1, a1, a3) before using their unkillable ability.
Seeker has a 1 turn delay (a1, a2) before using the turn meter boost ability.




Brutal:

Razelvarg opens a3, then a2.  This can be prioritized in the team setup.
Maneater (Fast) has a 1 turn delay (a1, a3) before using their unkillable ability.
Maneater (Slow) has a 3 turn delay (a1, a1, a1, a3) before using their unkillable ability.
Seeker has no delay, opening with their a2 turn meter boosting skill.
Slow Debuff landing on Seeker will not affect the tune.



Gear, Masteries, Blessings:



Gear

Any sets that don’t affect turn meter or skill timing for the key players (Razz, both Maneaters).  No relentless.

Razelvarg Specific:  No Gear that can grant Counter-Attack, Turn Meter, Buff Extension, or Skill Reduction.


Masteries

Stick to typical Masteries that won’t affect Turn Meter or skill timing.
Razelvarg Specific:  No Masteries that can grant Counter-Attack, Turn Meter, Buff Extension, or Skill Reduction.


Blessings

Stick to typical Clan Boss Blessings.
