## Notes

Affinity friendly single demytha unkillable team! What more can you ask for? Utilizing Heiress and two Painkeepers to keep the buffs up 100% of the time and one DPS to do your damage. 2 key UNM and 1 key Nightmare Clan Boss potential!


Reddit post: https://www.reddit.com/r/RaidShadowLegends/comments/u4gjcu/budget_demytha_2_key_unm_with_random_gear_and_a/


Works for UNM & NM with different setups.



ALL SPEEDS LISTED ARE TRUE SPEEDS.
Speeds listed exclude any speed aura.  Speed Auras can be used to lower the BUILD SPEEDS.
Use the calculator to check speeds and take into account any Area Bonuses your account may have.
Make sure to prioritize all the skills in the Team Setup.
Full Auto with AI presets.

Speed Ranges:


As with nearly every Speed Tune, there are more speeds that will work than are listed, but these are the ones tested that work best and most consistently for the approved difficulties and affinities.  Generally changing the speeds of one champion can change the speeds needed of other champions.  Feel free to play around in the calculator to find what works best for your team.  There are wider ranges and alternative speeds for just one difficulty. 





255-263 – Demytha
243-254 – Heiress
234-242 – DPS

Must be slower than Heiress


189 – Fast Painkeeper
188 – Slow Painkeeper



Speed Tune Sync:



UNM tune will sync on turn 3
NM tune will sync on turn 7

Skill Set Up:



UNM

Demytha: Open A3, then A2
Heiress: Open A2
Fast Painkeeper: Open A1, then A2, then A3
Slow Painkeeper: Open A3, then A2
This is a full auto setup when skills are pre-set.


NM

Demytha: Open A3, then A2
Heiress: Open A2
Fast Painkeeper: Open A2, then A3
Slow Painkeeper: Open A1, then A2, then A3
This is a full auto setup when skills are pre-set.



Gear, Masteries, Blessings:



Gear

Any sets that don’t affect turn meter or skill timing for the key players (Demytha/Heiress/Painkeepers).  No relentless.
No Counter Attack masteries or Accessories on the Painkeepers.


Masteries

Stick to typical Masteries that won’t affect Turn Meter or skill timing.


Blessings

Stick to typical Clan Boss Blessings. ( 1 Brimstone, 1 Cruelty, Remainder Phantom Touch )



Advanced Notes:



AI must be set on Demytha, Heiress and Painkeepers otherwise team will fail.

SPIRIT NOTES

Heiress True Speed > ( ( Slow PK True Speed * 9.1 ) + ( 1000 / 7 ) ) / 7.65

Demytha True Speed > ( ( Fast PK True Speed * 7.8 ) + ( 1000 / 7 ) ) / 6.35

This is only for UNM currently
