## Notes

Double Demytha’s Double the fun! Block damage or cleanse the stun!


Unfortunately this is NOT affinity friendly natively: to be affinity friendly for spirit affinity you need to bring in a cleanser (doompriest/tuhanarak) or debuff blocker (on a 3 turn cooldown).

Other Notes:


Be VERY careful utilizing Debuff Blockers for this team.  Additional buffs (like Increase Defense and Reflect Damage) can ruin stun targeting, as the clan boss likes to avoid those buffs, if they aren’t on everyone when the stun comes.  Even a 2 turn Block Debuff (or Increase Defense buff) can fall off of the Demytha’s if skill timing isn’t ideal.  If using a Block Debuff champ that does bring those additional buffs, it’s crucial to use the full auto setups (where the buff extension of Demytha happens in the middle of the turn order) and that should ensure the proper buffs are extended to cover everyone.  The Clan Boss doesn’t care about Block Debuffs when choosing stun targets, so even a 1 Turn Block Debuff can work as long as the stun goes to the covered DPS – you just have to ensure that the other buffs (Increase Defense/Reflect Damage) are on everyone or the Clan Boss will target those without it.

Demytha Specific:  Ideal timing has Demytha a2 happening at the top of each turn, but there’s no way to pre-set skills for full auto in those scenarios.  It can be manualed, which would allow Demytha to cleanse the stun negating the need to bring in a cleanser for that specific job.  This also opens up the ability to utilize additional cleansers besides doompriest (as long as it’s on a 3 turn cooldown) for the affinity debuffs.
