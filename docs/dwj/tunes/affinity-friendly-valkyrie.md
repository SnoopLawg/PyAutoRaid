# Affinity Friendly Valkyrie

**Type**: Traditional · **Difficulty**: Moderate · **Key**: 2 Key UNM · **Affinity**: All Affinities · **Author**: Beefeaterz

Source: https://deadwoodjedi.com/speed-tunes/affinity-friendly-valkyrie/

Speed ranges to maintain turn order with Valkyrie's Passive against affinity or void Clan Boss. Results will vary depending on your champions you use but speeds are quite low and should be a good entry point for people to challenge the Clan Boss.

## Slots

| # | Hero | SPD min | SPD max | Mastery | Relentless | CoM | LG |

| 1 | Cleanser/DPS | 189 | 189 | Warmaster | N | Y | Y |
| 2 | DPS | 187 | 189 | Warmaster | N | Y | - |
| 3 | DPS | 187 | 189 | Warmaster | N | Y | Y |
| 4 | DPS | 187 | 189 | Warmaster | N | Y | Y |
| 5 | Valkyrie | 171 | 172 | Warmaster | N | N | N |

## Slot Rules

### Slot 1 · Cleanser/DPS

Goes first in the turn order to cleanse the stun or other debuffs

## Notes

Vakyrie must be 171-172 and the rest of the team between 187 and 189, with one champion over 188. This will allow Valkyrie to go last each turn while the rest of the team goes before her. Speeds are pretty tight without a lot of leeway.


Regardless of affinity this turn order will maintain.


Works the same on NM as on UNM
