# Slow Hellcat Unkillable

**Type**: Unkillable · **Difficulty**: Easy · **Key**: 2 Key UNM · **Affinity**: All Affinities · **Author**: DeadwoodJedi

Source: https://deadwoodjedi.com/speed-tunes/slow-hellcat-unkillable/

Using the original Tower/Skullcrusher Unkillable, but utilizing Helicath to block damage on the clan boss AOE hits and his Shield to prevent dying from the stun.  Allows a cleanser or debuff blocker keep your team stun friendly against affinity CB.

## Slot Rules

### Slot 1 · Helicath

Needs a2 and a3 booked to 4 turn cooldowns.

### Slot 2 · Doompriest

The 186 will always go first after AOE2 to cleanse debuffs and/or to cleanse the Stun. Doompriest can cleanse both and as such is an ideal fit to make this affinity friendly.


If you’re using a Debuff Blocker instead of a Cleanser, you can use an additional DPS in this spot instead.

### Slot 3 · DPS

While 175 to 179 works. I highly recommend you hit 179 for this DPS so it works on both NM and UNM without too many issues with desync’s

### Slot 5 · Debuff Blocker

If you’re using a Cleanser instead of a Debuff Blocker, you can use this slot for additional DPS.

Gear, Masteries, Blessings:



Gear

Any sets that don’t affect turn meter or skill timing for the key players (Helicath/Block Debuff/Cleanse).
No relentless.


Masteries

Stick to typical Masteries that won’t affect Turn Meter or skill timing.


Blessings

Stick to typical Clan Boss Blessings.  ( 1 Brimstone, 1 Cruelty, Remainder Phantom Touch )



Advanced Notes:



Stun Adjustments

Helicath’s shield will drop off the slowest champ before the stun so they should not take the stun hit.
Against NM Heli will not be cleansed so the stun must not hit him.


Affinity Adjustments

When using a 1 turn block debuffs champ, you have to swap the 186 champ to 174 on Ultra Nightmare to stay affinity friendly.

This does not need to be done for Nightmare.
