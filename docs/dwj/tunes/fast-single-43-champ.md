# Fast Single 4:3 Champion

**Type**: Traditional · **Difficulty**: Easy · **Key**: 3 Key UNM · **Affinity**: All Affinities

Source: https://deadwoodjedi.com/speed-tunes/fast-single-43-champ/

1 Champ moves at a 4:3 ratio, taking a double turn on Ultra-Nightmare and Nightmare.  Perfect for champions with 4 turn cooldowns!

## Slot Rules

### Slot 1 · 4:3 Champion

Speed will determine where their double turn occurs.  Generally from 256-259 and 260-261 offer different rotations.

### Slot 2 · DPS

Speed will determine where their double turn occurs.  Generally from 195-204, 205-214, and 215-224 offer different rotations.

### Slot 3 · DPS

Faster speeds can change the turn order (over 195) so check in the calculator.

### Slot 5 · DPS

Goes last in the turn order (depending on the other speeds) and is a good spot for a Counter-Attack champion.
