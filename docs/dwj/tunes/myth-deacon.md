# Myth Deacon

**Type**: Unkillable · **Difficulty**: Hard · **Key**: 1 Key UNM · **Affinity**: All Affinities · **Author**: TheDragonsBeard

Source: https://deadwoodjedi.com/speed-tunes/myth-deacon/

This speed tune is ideal for anyone looking to utilise Demytha in a 2:1 Fully Unkillable Comp without Seeker. It uses Deacon Armstrong and High Khatun to fill Turn Meter and Increase Speed whilst giving 2 spots for damage. 

Speeds are quite high so will be challenging gear wise.

## Slots

| # | Hero | SPD min | SPD max | Mastery | Relentless | CoM | LG |
|---|------|---------|---------|---------|------------|-----|-----|
| 1 | Demytha | 310 | - | Warmaster | Y | N | N |
| 2 | High Khatun | 309 | 310 | Warmaster | N | N | - |
| 3 | Deacon Armstrong | 196 | 200 | Warmaster | N | N | N |
| 4 | DPS | 202 | 203 | Warmaster | N | N | N |
| 5 | DPS | 172 | 179 | Warmaster | N | N | N |

## Slot Rules

### Slot 4 · DPS

True Speed must be less than Seeker’s True Speed * (7.15/7.8)

## Notes

Displayed speeds include High Khatun’s Speed Aura

Team is NOT naturally affinity friendly. Stun must go to one of the DPS.


w/ Brogni Affinity Friendly – DPS or Brogni must take the 1st stun, in addition you will need to add a delay of 1 turn to the block debuff ability on turn 3 after the stun, so that’s it’s used again on turn 4, between the aoe’s.
