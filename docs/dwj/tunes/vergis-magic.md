# Vergis Magic

**Type**: Traditional · **Difficulty**: Easy · **Key**: 3 Key UNM · **Affinity**: All Affinities · **Author**: DeadwoodJedi

Source: https://deadwoodjedi.com/speed-tunes/vergis-magic/

Vergis speeds up one champ to a 4:3 turn ratio, going First/Last between AOEs. Perfect Beginner Speed Tune for Clan Boss! Works on UNM and NM!

## Slot Rules

### Slot 1 · 4:3 Champion

This is an ideal role for a block debuff champion on a 4 turn CD like Sepulcher Sentinel.


UNM

1-turn delay on 4:3 champion skill.


NM

2-turn delay on 4:3 champion skill.

### Slot 2 · Counter-Attacker

191-194 champ will be stunned (unless affinity trumps) on first stun attack, so adjust accordingly.

### Slot 4 · Vergis

Should be faster than last DPS slot

## Notes

Works for UNM and NM.



ALL SPEEDS LISTED ARE TRUE SPEEDS.
Speeds listed exclude any speed aura.  Speed Auras can be used to lower the BUILD SPEEDS.
Use the calculator to check speeds and take into account any Area Bonuses your account may have.
Make sure to prioritize all the skills in the Team Setup.

Speed Ranges:


As with nearly every Speed Tune, there are more speeds that will work than are listed, but these are the ones tested that work best and most consistently for the approved difficulties and affinities.  Generally changing the speeds of one champion can change the speeds needed of other champions.  Feel free to play around in the calculator to find what works best for your team.  There are wider ranges and alternative speeds for just one difficulty. 





205-201 – 4:3 Champion
191-194 – Counter Attack Champion
179-182 – DPS
172-177 – Vergis

Vergis should be faster than slow DPS


172-175 – DPS



Speed Tune Sync:



UNM tune will sync on turn 3
NM tune will sync on turn 7

Skill Set Up:



UNM

4:3 Champion: Open A1, then 4 Turn Cooldown Skill
Counter Attack: Open A1, then A1, then A1, then Counter Attack Skill

This champ will take the 1st stun if using Sepulcher
If not using Sepulcher adjust Counter Attack skill as necessary


Vergis: Open A2


NM

4:3 Champion: Open A1, then A1, then 4 Turn Cooldown Skill
Counter Attack: Open with Counter Attack Skill

This champ will take the 2nd stun if using Sepulcher
If not using Sepulcher adjust Counter Attack skill as necessary


Vergis: Open A1, then A2



Gear, Masteries, Blessings:



Gear

Any sets that don’t affect turn meter or skill timing for the key players (4:3 Champ/Vergis/Counter Attack Champ).  No relentless.


Masteries

Stick to typical Masteries that won’t affect Turn Meter or skill timing.


Blessings

Stick to typical Clan Boss Blessings.  ( 1 Brimstone, 1 Cruelty, Remainder Phantom Touch )



Advanced Notes:



Works especially well with a Sepulcher Sentinel as the 4:3 champion and Skullcrusher as counter-attack champion. As always rotation may vary if true speeds differ.
