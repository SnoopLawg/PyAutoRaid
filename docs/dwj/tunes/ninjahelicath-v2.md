# NinjaHelicath v2

**Type**: Unkillable · **Difficulty**: Moderate · **Key**: 2 Key UNM · **Affinity**: All Affinities · **Author**: Lil

Source: https://deadwoodjedi.com/speed-tunes/ninjahelicath-v2/

Utilizing Helicath to block damage on the clan boss AOE hits and his Shield to prevent dying from the stun and Ninja for insane damage. Allows a debuff blocker keep your team stun friendly against affinity CB

## Notes

Ninja of course is an amazing damage dealer, but MAKE SURE to read the guidance below as he can easily mess up this team depending on skill priority and affinity.  As always check first in the calculator!!

Helicath will block the damage for the AOE’s and use his shield to keep your stun target alive. You will want as much defense on Helicath as you can build.


Tested this on all affinities now (and with a few different champs – including Skullcrusher), works great. I did disable Tatura’s A3 to keep the stun target consistent. You can also bring in Ally Attack and still stay in tune.

On Nightmare replace Ninja with a 186 (186-190) DPS
