# Man Salad

**Type**: Unkillable · **Difficulty**: Moderate · **Key**: 1 Key UNM · **Affinity**: All Affinities · **Author**: DeadwoodJedi

Source: https://deadwoodjedi.com/speed-tunes/man-salad/

Using Double Maneater and Ma'Shalled to achieve an affinity friendly 2:1 unkillable team.

## Slot Rules

### Slot 1 · Ma'Shalled

A1, A2

### Slot 2 · Maneater

A2, A1, A3, …don’t use A3 again until his 2nd attack of turn count 5

### Slot 3 · Maneater

A2, A1, A2, A1, A1, A3 (A3 should be at the start of turn count 4)

### Slot 5 · Pain Keeper

A3, A1, A1, A2

## Notes

Using Double Maneater and Ma’Shalled to achieve an affinity friendly 2:1 unkillable team.



ALL SPEEDS LISTED ARE TRUE SPEEDS.
Speeds listed exclude any speed aura.  Speed Auras can be used to lower the BUILD SPEEDS.
Use the calculator to check speeds and take into account any Area Bonuses your account may have.
Make sure to prioritize all the skills in the Team Setup.

Speed Ranges:


As with nearly every Speed Tune, there are more speeds that will work than are listed, but these are the ones tested that work best and most consistently for the approved difficulties and affinities.  Generally changing the speeds of one champion can change the speeds needed of other champions.  Feel free to play around in the calculator to find what works best for your team.  There are wider ranges and alternative speeds for just one difficulty.

250.55-252 – Painkeeper

186-192 – DPS

Skill Set Up:



UNM / NM / Brutal

Ma’Shalled: Open A3, then A2
Painkeeper: Open A3, then A1, then A1, then A2
Fast ManEater: Open A2, then A3
Slow ManEater: Open A2, then A1, then A3


Affinity & Stun Friendly Setup

Ma’Shalled: Open A3, then A2
Painkeeper: Open A3, then A1, then A1, then A2
Fast ManEater: Open A2, then A1, then A1, then A3, then A2, then A1, then A1, then A2, then A1, then A3
Slow ManEater: Open A2, then A3, then A1, then A2, then A1, then A2, then A3



Gear, Masteries, Blessings:



Gear

Any sets that don’t affect turn meter or skill timing for the key players (Ma’Shalled/ManEaters/Painkeeper).
No relentless.
No Counter Attack accessories on Painkeeper.


Masteries

Stick to typical Masteries that won’t affect Turn Meter or skill timing.
No Counter Attack Masteries on Painkeeper.


Blessings

Stick to typical Clan Boss Blessings.  ( 1 Brimstone, 1 Cruelty, Remainder Phantom Touch )



Advanced Notes:



So long as the stun does NOT target Ma’Shalled or Pain Keeper, this team can hit auto after Pain Keeper’s A2 right before the first stun.
Longer setup is affinity and stun friendly, but need everyone to survive until Turn 5.

201-212 DPS

Original Setup

UNM Setup (all affinity friendly):

Ma’Shalled – A1, A2
Fast Maneater – A2, A1, A3, …don’t use A3 again until his 2nd attack of turn count 5
Slow Maneater – A2, A1, A2, A1, A1, A3 (A3 should be at the start of turn count 4)
Pain Keeper – A3, A1, A1, A2

Auto after Fast Maneater’s A3 on turn count 5.
First stun is unprotected. Do NOT let Ma’Shalled or the Slow Maneater get hit by it.




NM Setup (all affinity friendly):

Ma’Shalled – A1, A2
Fast Maneater – A2, A1, A1, A3
Slow Maneater – A2, A1, A3, A2, A1, A2, A1, A1, A3 (this A3 should be the Maneater’s first action of turn count 5)
Pain Keeper – A2, A1, A1, A3

Auto after Slow Maneater’s A3 on turn count 5.
First stun is unprotected for Ma’Shalled; do NOT let him get hit by it.
