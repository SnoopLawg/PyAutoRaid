# Razzle Dazzle Counter-Attack

**Type**: Traditional · **Difficulty**: Moderate · **Key**: 1 Key UNM · **Affinity**: All Affinities · **Author**: ShortonSkillz

Source: https://deadwoodjedi.com/speed-tunes/razzle-dazzle-counter-attack/

Clan Boss team focused around the crazy speeds of Razelvarg and a Counter-Attacker to go incredibly fast and do great damage!

## Slot Rules

### Slot 1 · Razelvarg

Razelvarg opens a2, then a3.  This can be prioritized in the team setup.


You CANNOT use Counter-Attack on Razelvarg.  Ally-Attack’s must be used immediately before Razelvarg takes his turn.


Razelvarg Specific:  No Masteries or Gear that can grant Counter-Attack, Turn Meter, Buff Extension, or Skill Reduction.

### Slot 2 · Buff Extender

Ideal slot for Protection (Ally-Protection, Increase Def, Strengthen, etc) Champion.

### Slot 5 · Counter-Attack

The first stun will not be cleansed, so plan your skills accordingly.

## Notes

Works for both UNM and NM.  When utilizing Razelvargs speed aura this is arguably the slowest 2:1 ratio teams in the game.



ALL SPEEDS LISTED ARE TRUE SPEEDS.
Speeds listed exclude any speed aura. If you use an aura you should reduce your build speed accordingly.
Use the calculator to find the build speed and take into account any Area Bonuses your account may have.
True speeds do not require you to add bonus speed from Razelvarg passive. The build speeds factor in his passive and is calculated automatically by the calculator.
Speeds are very closely linked together.  Changing one champs speeds may alter the order of the champs or the tune itself.  ALWAYS DOUBLE CHECK YOUR SETUP IN THE CALCULATOR.

Speed Ranges:


As with nearly every Speed Tune, there are more speeds that will work than are listed, but these are the ones tested that work best and most consistently for the approved difficulties and affinities.  Generally changing the speeds of one champion can change the speeds needed of other champions.  Feel free to play around in the calculator to find what works best for your team.





258-263 Razelvarg ( 236-241 w/ Razel’s Aura )
263 Buff Extender
251-252 Block Debuff
220-228 DPS
198-204 Counter Attack



Buff Extender MUST be for 2 turn duration on 3 turn cooldown. Examples are Pythion, Brogni, Cardiel.


Speed Tune Sync:



UNM tune will sync on turn 6
NM tune will sync on turn 8
Brutal will sync on turn 9

Skill Set Up:



UNM / NM

Razelvarg: Open A1, then A2, then A3
Buff Extender: Open A1, then Buff Extension skill
Block Debuff: Open with Block Debuff skill
Counter Attack: Open with A1, then Counter Attack Skill


Brutal

Razelvarg: Open A1, then A3
Buff Extender: Open A1, then Buff Extension skill
Block Debuff: Open with Block Debuff skill
Counter Attack: Open with A1, then Counter Attack Skill



Gear, Masteries, Blessings:



Gear

Any sets that don’t affect turn meter or skill timing for the key players (Razz, Counter-Attack, Cleanser).  No relentless.

Razelvarg Specific:  No Gear that can grant Turn Meter, Buff Extension, or Skill Reduction.  No Counter Attack Accessories or Gear.


Masteries

Stick to typical Masteries that won’t affect Turn Meter or skill timing.

No Cooldown Reduction Masteries on anyone but the DPS.


Razelvarg Specific:  No Masteries that can grant Turn Meter, Buff Extension, Skill Reduction, or Counter Attack.


Blessings

Stick to typical Clan Boss Blessings. ( 1 Brimstone, 1 Cruelty, Remainder Phantom Touch )



Advanced Notes:



Razelvarg must not take the stun.

Give Razel the Steadfast mastery. ( Tier 1 Support )
Use a Magic affinity champion to be the stun target against Force Affinity.
