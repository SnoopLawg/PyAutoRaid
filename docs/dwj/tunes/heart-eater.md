# Heart Eater

**Type**: Unkillable · **Difficulty**: Moderate · **Key**: 2 Key UNM · **Affinity**: All Affinities · **Author**: SIpho879

Source: https://deadwoodjedi.com/speed-tunes/heart-eater/

## Notes

Works for both UNM and NM. Emic Trunkheart will move at a 4:3 Speed Ratio, enabling him to keep unkillable on your full team for the first AOE attack while cooling down your allies abilities – specifically Maneater – allowing the usage of Maneater’s AOE unkillable ability to stay protected for the second AOE and Stun and stay affinity friendly.



ALL SPEEDS LISTED ARE TRUE SPEEDS.
Speeds listed exclude any speed aura. If you use an aura you should reduce your build speed accordingly.
Use the calculator to find the build speed and take into account any Area Bonuses your account may have.
Make sure to prioritize all the skills in the Team Setup
Full Auto on UNM, but not NM

Speed Ranges:


As with nearly every Speed Tune, there are more speeds that will work than are listed, but these are the ones tested that work best and most consistently for the approved difficulties and affinities.  Generally changing the speeds of one champion can change the speeds needed of other champions.  Feel free to play around in the calculator to find what works best for your team.



CLEANSER

245-254 – Emic Trunkheart
239-244 – Maneater
170-178 – DPS
170-178 – DPS
170-178 – DPS



Maneater’s A3 needs to be fully booked (5 turn cooldown)

Emic’s A3 needs to be fully booked (4 turn cooldown), his A2 does not (4 turn cooldown)


Speed Tune Sync:



UNM tune will sync on turn 2
NM tune will sync on turn 2

Emic will take a double turn before the first AOE attack, placing unkillable on his second attack.  Maneater will take a double turn before the second AOE attack, placing unkillable and block debuffs on his second attack.


Skill Set Up:



UNM

Emic opens a3, then a2.
Maneater opens a1, then a3.
This is a full auto setup when skills are pre-set.


NM

Emic opens a1, a3, then a2.
Maneater has a 2 turn delay on his a3 skill:  a1, a1, a3
NOT full auto.  Must manual Maneater’s second attack (the a1), but can auto after that.



Gear, Masteries, Blessings:



Gear

Any sets that don’t affect turn meter or skill timing for the key players (both Unkillable/Block Damage Champs, Cleanser, Block Debuff).  No relentless.


Masteries

Stick to typical Masteries that won’t affect Turn Meter or skill timing.


Blessings

Stick to typical Clan Boss Blessings.



Advanced Notes:



Due to Emic’s a2 skill reducing cooldowns, you can use champs with 4 turn cd’s to great effect
