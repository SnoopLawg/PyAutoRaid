## Notes

Simple 1:1 ratio speed tune which is the foundation for every other speed tune we’ve created.



ALL SPEEDS LISTED ARE TRUE SPEEDS.
Speeds listed exclude any speed aura.  Speed Auras can be used to lower the BUILD SPEEDS.
Use the calculator to check speeds and take into account any Area Bonuses your account may have.
Make sure to prioritize all the skills in the Team Setup.

Speed Ranges:


As with nearly every Speed Tune, there are more speeds that will work than are listed, but these are the ones tested that work best and most consistently for the approved difficulties and affinities.  Generally changing the speeds of one champion can change the speeds needed of other champions.  Feel free to play around in the calculator to find what works best for your team.  There are wider ranges and alternative speeds for just one difficulty. 





If you want your team starting before Clan Boss, All Champions: 191-209
​If you want your team starting after Clan Boss, All Champions: 171-189



Speed Tune Sync:



UNM tune will sync on turn 1
NM tune will sync on turn 1

Gear, Masteries, Blessings:



Gear

Any sets that don’t affect turn meter or skill timing for the key players.
No relentless.


Masteries

Stick to typical Masteries that won’t affect Turn Meter or skill timing.


Blessings

Stick to typical Clan Boss Blessings.  ( 1 Brimstone, 1 Cruelty, Remainder Phantom Touch )



Advanced Notes:



These two ranges work with each other for UNM.​  These speeds can work well in Nightmare as well, but the closer you are to the speed of the Clan Boss the better.  Differences of about 20 points or so of the CB speed can result in lapping of the boss (or yourself) and alterations of the turn order.
When mixing these speeds, on Ultra-Nightmare, they are roughly equivalent to the speed with a 20 points difference. Between the two the higher one will go first. So 191 will go before 171, but both will go after 172 and higher.  The same concept applies to Nightmare, though the speeds required are lower and generally revolve around the speed of the Nightmare Clan Boss (170).
