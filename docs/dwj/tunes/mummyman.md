## Notes

This is MummyMan speed tune, which is affinity friendly and works on both UNM and NM (but not Brutal).



ALL SPEEDS LISTED ARE TRUE SPEEDS.
Speeds listed exclude any speed aura.  Speed Auras can be used to lower the BUILD SPEEDS.
Use the calculator to check speeds and take into account any Area Bonuses your account may have.
Make sure to prioritize all the skills in the Team Setup.

Speed Ranges:


As with nearly every Speed Tune, there are more speeds that will work than are listed, but these are the ones tested that work best and most consistently for the approved difficulties and affinities.  Generally changing the speeds of one champion can change the speeds needed of other champions.  Feel free to play around in the calculator to find what works best for your team.  There are wider ranges and alternative speeds for just one difficulty.





302-309 – Maneater
274-284 – Khafru
191-194 – Rathalos
179-182 – DPS
126-129 – High Khatun (any 3 turn Speed Booster with 30% speed buff and 15% tm boost will work here, just lack the aura)



Speed Tune Sync:



UNM tune will sync on turn 3
NM tune will sync on turn 3

Skill Set Up:



UNM / NM

Maneater: Prioritize A3, but Open with A1 or A2
Rathalos: Open with A1, Prioritize A3, then A2
Khafru: Prioritize A3, but needs 3 turn delay before using for first time (A1, A1, A1, A3)
High Khatun: Prioritize A2, but Open with A1 or A3
Full Auto after using Khafru’s A3 skill.


Maneater and Khafru will both be on a 5:3 ratio, allowing Khafru to take the stun without a cleanser and Maneater to keep the team unkillable for the AOE’s.
Rathalos and DPS will take 4 or 3 turns per cycle.  They run a little random.
You can use another DPS instead of Rathalos on UNM, but not NM.

Gear, Masteries, Blessings:



Gear

Any sets that don’t affect turn meter or skill timing for the key players (Maneater/High Khatun/ Rathalos/Khafru).
No Relentless


Masteries

Stick to typical Masteries that won’t affect Turn Meter or skill timing.


Blessings

Stick to typical Clan Boss Blessings.  ( 1 Brimstone, 1 Cruelty, Remainder Phantom Touch )



Advanced Notes:



If you don’t have Rathalos, you can run a typical CB DPS in his slot on UNM

Doesn’t work for NM


All named Champions must have their key skills booked fully.
