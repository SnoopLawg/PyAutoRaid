# Double Warcaster v2

**Type**: Unkillable · **Difficulty**: Moderate · **Key**: 2 Key UNM · **Affinity**: All Affinities · **Author**: Cyborg

Source: https://deadwoodjedi.com/speed-tunes/double-warcaster-v2/

Double Warcaster blocks the AOEs and Skullcrusher or any champ that won't die easily takes the stun.

## Notes

Works for UNM and NM



ALL SPEEDS LISTED ARE TRUE SPEEDS.
Speeds listed exclude any speed aura.  Speed Auras can be used to lower the BUILD SPEEDS.
Use the calculator to check speeds and take into account any Area Bonuses your account may have.
Make sure to prioritize all the skills in the Team Setup.

Speed Ranges:


As with nearly every Speed Tune, there are more speeds that will work than are listed, but these are the ones tested that work best and most consistently for the approved difficulties and affinities.  Generally changing the speeds of one champion can change the speeds needed of other champions.  Feel free to play around in the calculator to find what works best for your team.  There are wider ranges and alternative speeds for just one difficulty. 





245-249 – Fast Warcaster
219-238 – 4:3 Champion ( UK Stun Target or DPS )
218-219– Slow Warcaster

True Speed must be Higher than 4:3 DPS
Speeds are very tight to work on both UNM & NM

Double Check Calculator to see how sets effect the tune




218 – 4:3 DPS or 2 Turn Block Debuff champ on 4 turn Cooldown
190 – 1:1 DPS or 2 Turn Block Debuff champ on 3 Turn Cooldown



Speed Tune Sync:



UNM / NM tune will sync on turn 1

Skill Set Up:



UNM / NM

Fast Warcaster: Open A1, then A1, then A3
Slow Warcaster: Open A3
Block Debuff Champion: Open A1, then Block Debuff Skill
Skullcrusher: Open A2



Gear, Masteries, Blessings:



Gear

Any sets that don’t affect turn meter or skill timing for the key players (Warcasters/Block Debuffs/Skullcrusher).
Relentless is okay on DPS.


Masteries

Stick to typical Masteries that won’t affect Turn Meter or skill timing.
No Deterrence Mastery on Skullcrusher if using.


Blessings

Stick to typical Clan Boss Blessings.  ( 1 Brimstone, 1 Cruelty, Remainder Phantom Touch )



Advanced Notes:



Both DPS are unprotected for 1st AOE.
Non Unkillable Stun Target should be built with 4k+ Defense in Lifesteal gear.
