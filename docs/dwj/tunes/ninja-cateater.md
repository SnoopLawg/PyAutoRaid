# Ninja CatEater

**Type**: Unkillable · **Difficulty**: Moderate · **Key**: 2 Key UNM · **Affinity**: All Affinities · **Author**: Xaavik, Facemelter, Optilink

Source: https://deadwoodjedi.com/speed-tunes/ninja-cateater/

Unkillable Team utilizing Ninja, along with Helicath and Maneater to stay unkillable and affinity friendly.

1 Key UNM is possible with very good gear and meta champions.

## Slot Rules

### Slot 2 · Seeker

If Seeker takes the first stun, simply use his a1 on his next available attack (turning a 3 turn delay into a 5 turn delay on his a2)

### Slot 3 · Ninja

Ninja’s skills must be prioritized in order a3, a2, a1.  Open with his a2 on all difficulties.  This means his order will be a2, a3, a2, a1, a1

Ninja will not work for Force (red) affinity – substitute a champion at 214 speed (like normal CatEater).

On Brutal difficulty he cannot take the stun.  He should have veil up though, so it should be fine.

Cannot take the counter attack masteries in the defensive tree (best to simply go support tree).  Will not work with counter attack.

Ally Attacks must be timed to happen right before the stun on all difficulties.  Make sure to time those skills.

Gear, Masteries, Blessings:



Gear

Any sets that don’t affect turn meter or skill timing for the key players (ManEater/Seeker/Block Damage/Ninja).  No relentless.
No Counter Attack Accessories or Gear for Ninja.


Masteries

Stick to typical Masteries that won’t affect Turn Meter or skill timing.
No Counter Attack Masteries for Ninja.


Blessings

Stick to typical Clan Boss Blessings.  ( 1 Brimstone, 1 Cruelty, Remainder Phantom Touch )



Advanced Notes:



You’ll want to avoid the stun hitting Maneater or Helicath on the first stun.  Place them last in the turn order and ensure you have someone of weak and/or neutral affinity ahead of them in the team order.
Ninja

AI must be prioritized as A3 – 1st choice, A2 – 2nd choice.
No CA gear, accessories or masteries.


Ally Attack DPS can be used.  Ally Attack skill must be timed to go right before the stun.
