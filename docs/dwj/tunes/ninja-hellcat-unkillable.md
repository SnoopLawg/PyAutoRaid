# Ninja Hellcat Unkillable

**Type**: Unkillable · **Difficulty**: Moderate · **Key**: 2 Key UNM · **Affinity**: All Affinities · **Author**: Tiago Titan

Source: https://deadwoodjedi.com/speed-tunes/ninja-hellcat-unkillable/

Utilizing Helicath to block damage on the clan boss AOE hits and his Shield to prevent dying from the stun and Ninja for insane damage.  Allows a cleanser or debuff blocker keep your team stun friendly against affinity CB.

## Slot Rules

### Slot 1 · Helicath

Needs a2 and a3 booked to 4 turn cooldowns.

Should not take the Stun on Nightmare when using a cleanser.

### Slot 2 · Doompriest

The 186 will always go first after AOE2 to cleanse debuffs and/or to cleanse the Stun. Doompriest can cleanse both and as such is an ideal fit to make this affinity friendly.


If you’re using a Debuff Blocker instead of a Cleanser, you can use an additional DPS in this spot instead.

### Slot 3 · DPS

Must be faster than Ninja

### Slot 4 · Ninja

Cannot be the slowest Champion.


Ninja MUST CRIT on his first A1 on UNM and BOTH of his first two A1 attacks on Nightmare.  If he does that successfully he can be used against weak affinity.

I believe with the following setup he can be used with his AI priorities (A2 first, A3 second) which will significantly up his damage.  However, this is not yet confirmed, so the safer bet is to prioritize his A3 over his A2 skill.


For UNM: A3, A2, A1, Auto (this can be preset to run full auto from the start – just confirm the crit on his A1)

For NM: A1, A1, Auto (you will have to manual his first two attacks then you can run Auto after that.  Those first two attacks MUST crit.)

### Slot 5 · Debuff Blocker

Must be slower than Ninja


If you’re using a Cleanser instead of a Debuff Blocker, you can use this slot for additional DPS.

Gear, Masteries, Blessings:



Gear

Any sets that don’t affect turn meter or skill timing for the key players (Helicath/Ninja/Block Debuff/Cleanse).
No relentless.
No Counter Attack Accessories or Gear on Ninja.


Masteries

Stick to typical Masteries that won’t affect Turn Meter or skill timing.
No Counter Attack Masteries on Ninja.


Blessings

Stick to typical Clan Boss Blessings.  ( 1 Brimstone, 1 Cruelty, Remainder Phantom Touch )



Advanced Notes:



Ninja MUST NOT WEAK HIT on his First A1 on UNM and BOTH of his First two A1 attacks on Nightmare.  If he does that successfully he can be used against Force Affinity Clan Boss.
Stun Adjustments

Helicath’s shield will drop off the slowest champ before the stun so they should not take the stun hit.
Against NM Heli will not be cleansed so the stun must not hit him.
