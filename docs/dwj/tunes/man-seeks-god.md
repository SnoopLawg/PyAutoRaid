## Notes

Using Revive on Death mechanic with Maneater to stay unkillable for 50 turns.



ALL SPEEDS LISTED ARE TRUE SPEEDS.
Speeds listed exclude any speed aura.  Speed Auras can be used to lower the BUILD SPEEDS.
Use the calculator to check speeds and take into account any Area Bonuses your account may have.
Make sure to prioritize all the skills in the Team Setup.

Speed Ranges:


As with nearly every Speed Tune, there are more speeds that will work than are listed, but these are the ones tested that work best and most consistently for the approved difficulties and affinities.  Generally changing the speeds of one champion can change the speeds needed of other champions.  Feel free to play around in the calculator to find what works best for your team.  There are wider ranges and alternative speeds for just one difficulty.

Advanced Notes:



The speeds need to be VERY precise and it is RECOMMENDED FOR EXPERT PLAYERS ONLY who are familiar with both speed tuning and the calculator.

One realistic example of a working setup (without a speed aura) is Seeker 2 speed sets, Maneater 3 speed sets, the 191 champions in no speed-affecting sets. In this particular case it doesn’t matter if ME and/or Seeker has Lore of Steel, but in general you have to take it into account (LoS is neither good nor bad, just another thing you need to input accurately in order to get accurate results).


The idea is that the stun eventually kills one of the 191 speed champions (typically your DPS) and either Aniri’s A3 or a revive-on-death buff revives them.
Godseeker Aniri

You must set Godseeker’s AI to use her A3 as first choice or disable her A2. By default she’d go A2 even if someone is dead.
One possible issue is Seeker proccing his passive on AoE2, then Aniri extending the increase defense buff so everybody but Seeker has it. You can disable Aniri’s A2 to avoid this or use a DPS with an Increase Defense skill.
Against Spirit open with Aniri’s A2 to extend the Block Debuffs.


Cardiel + Ninja

Note that even though the speeds seem to have a little more leeway than the standard tune, you still need to be able to substitute Ninja against force (and the non-Cardiel 190 DPS needs to be magic so that Seeker doesn’t become the stun target).
