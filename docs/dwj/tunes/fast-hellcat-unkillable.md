# Fast Hellcat Unkillable

**Type**: Unkillable · **Difficulty**: Moderate · **Key**: 2 Key UNM · **Affinity**: All Affinities · **Author**: DeadwoodJedi

Source: https://deadwoodjedi.com/speed-tunes/fast-hellcat-unkillable/

Unkillable team using Helicath to block damage on the clan boss AOE hits and his Shield to prevent dying from the stun.  This version has a 4:3 ratio champion to provide more damage.

1 Key UNM is possible with very good gear and meta champions.

## Notes

Unkillable team using Helicath to block damage on the clan boss AOE hits and his Shield to prevent dying from the stun. This version has a 4:3 ratio champion to provide more damage.



ALL SPEEDS LISTED ARE TRUE SPEEDS.
Speeds listed exclude any speed aura.  Speed Auras can be used to lower the BUILD SPEEDS.
Use the calculator to check speeds and take into account any Area Bonuses your account may have.
Make sure to prioritize all the skills in the Team Setup.

Speed Ranges:


As with nearly every Speed Tune, there are more speeds that will work than are listed, but these are the ones tested that work best and most consistently for the approved difficulties and affinities.  Generally changing the speeds of one champion can change the speeds needed of other champions.  Feel free to play around in the calculator to find what works best for your team.  There are wider ranges and alternative speeds for just one difficulty. 





243-245 – DPS
241 – Helicath
195-196 – DPS
193 – DPS / Alternate Debuff Blocker
191 – Debuff Blocker / Cleanser



Speed Tune Sync:



UNM tune will sync on turn 6
NM tune will sync on turn 9

Skill Set Up:



UNM

Helicath: Open with A3
Debuff Blocker: Adjust delays as necessary to block Affinity Debuff or Stun.


NM

Helicath: Open with A1, then A3
Debuff Blocker: Adjust delays as necessary to block Affinity Debuff or Stun.



Gear, Masteries, Blessings:



Gear

Any sets that don’t affect turn meter or skill timing for the key players (Helicath/Debuff Blocker/Cleanser).
No relentless.


Masteries

Stick to typical Masteries that won’t affect Turn Meter or skill timing.


Blessings

Stick to typical Clan Boss Blessings.  ( 1 Brimstone, 1 Cruelty, Remainder Phantom Touch )



Advanced Notes:



Helicath will block the damage for the AOE’s and use his shield to keep your stun target alive.  To maximize the shield ability, you will want increase defense before that skill is used (any champion but the 191 champion) as well as low HP (and/or high defense) on your stun target.
Affinity Adjustments

UNM:  You will need a block debuff champion with a 3 turn cooldown on that skill.  If you want to block the stun debuff as well, a 2 turn duration is required and you will want to avoid the stun going to your 4:3 champion.
NM:  You will need a cleanser or block debuff champion with a 3 turn cooldown and 2 turn duration on that skill.  The block debuff champion should be your stun target if you wish to avoid the stun debuff as well.
