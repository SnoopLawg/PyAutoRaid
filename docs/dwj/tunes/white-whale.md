## Notes

The White Whale was the first team to go at a consistent 2:1 ratio.  It can be done in 2 ways, with many variations since first discovered.  The two primary methods involve a speed booster (typically like High Khatun: 15% tm boost + 30% speed buff, though there is a version for champs like Lydia without the TM boost) and either another speed booster or a buff extender.  Both are listed below.



ALL SPEEDS LISTED ARE TRUE SPEEDS.
Speeds listed exclude any speed aura.  Speed Auras can be used to lower the BUILD SPEEDS.
Use the calculator to check speeds and take into account any Area Bonuses your account may have.
Make sure to prioritize all the skills in the Team Setup.

SPEED BOOSTER WITH BUFF EXTENDER


Speed Ranges:


As with nearly every Speed Tune, there are more speeds that will work than are listed, but these are the ones tested that work best and most consistently for the approved difficulties and affinities.  Generally changing the speeds of one champion can change the speeds needed of other champions.  Feel free to play around in the calculator to find what works best for your team.  There are wider ranges and alternative speeds for just one difficulty. 





268-281 – Speed Booster
245-248 – Buff Extender

True Speed must be Higher than DPS


244-247 – DPS

True Speed must be Lower than DPS


212-228 – DPS / Block Debuff
194-206 – DPS / Cleanse



Speed Tune Sync:



UNM tune will sync on turn 5
NM tune will sync on turn 5

Skill Set Up:



UNM / NM

Speed Booster: Open Speed Boost Skill
Buff Extender: Open Buff Extend Skill
Block Debuff / Cleanse: Adjust Skill delays as necessary



Gear, Masteries, Blessings:



Gear

Any sets that don’t affect turn meter or skill timing for the key players (Speed Booster/Buff Extender).
No relentless.


Masteries

Stick to typical Masteries that won’t affect Turn Meter or skill timing.


Blessings

Stick to typical Clan Boss Blessings.  ( 1 Brimstone, 1 Cruelty, Remainder Phantom Touch )



Advanced Notes:



Equal delays can be added to both the speed buff and buff extension to adjust where in the cycle you want buff extensions.

SPEED BOOSTER WITH 2ND SPEED CHAMPION


Speed Ranges:


As with nearly every Speed Tune, there are more speeds that will work than are listed, but these are the ones tested that work best and most consistently for the approved difficulties and affinities.  Generally changing the speeds of one champion can change the speeds needed of other champions.  Feel free to play around in the calculator to find what works best for your team.  There are wider ranges and alternative speeds for just one difficulty. 





268-292 – Fast Speed Booster
244-257 – Slow Speed Booster
220-225 – DPS
220-225 – DPS / Block Debuff
193-210 – DPS / Cleanse



Speed Tune Sync:



UNM tune will sync on turn 5
NM tune will sync on turn 5
Brutal tune will sync on turn 6

Skill Set Up:



UNM / NM / Brutal

Fast Speed Booster: Open Speed Boost Skill
Slow Speed Booster: Open A1, then Speed Boost Skill
Block Debuff / Cleanse: Adjust Skill delays as necessary



Gear, Masteries, Blessings:



Gear

Any sets that don’t affect turn meter or skill timing for the key players (Speed Booster/Buff Extender).
No relentless.


Masteries

Stick to typical Masteries that won’t affect Turn Meter or skill timing.


Blessings

Stick to typical Clan Boss Blessings.  ( 1 Brimstone, 1 Cruelty, Remainder Phantom Touch )



Advanced Notes:



Both Speed Boosters need to avoid being stunned.
Fastest champion goes last in turn order.
To be affinity friendly will need Cleanser such as Doompriest.
Debuff blocker can block either stun or affinity debuffs (if 2 turn duration) not both.
