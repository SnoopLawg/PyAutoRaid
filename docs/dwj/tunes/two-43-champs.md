## Slot Rules

### Slot 3 · DPS

When running this champion from 191-197 will typically go last in turn order if less than 20 speed faster than slowest champion.

## Notes

225-238 champs take a double turn.


All champ speeds affect each other and can determine when the double turns occur.


If 191-197 champ is less than 20 speed faster than slowest champ, typically they will go last in turn order; double turn champs will go last on the double turn. If slowest champ is 20+ speed slower than the 191-197 champ, typically they will go last every turn.  If between 171-189 instead, the slowest champ is last.


This is useful for having two champs on a 4-turn cooldown using their abilities every cycle while allowing your CA champion to still go last. As always, variations within ranges may alter the turn order.


Play with the numbers and find a comp that works for you!
