# CatEater

**Type**: Unkillable · **Difficulty**: Moderate · **Key**: 2 Key UNM · **Affinity**: All Affinities · **Author**: Xaavik, Facemelter, Optilink

Source: https://deadwoodjedi.com/speed-tunes/cateater/

Unkillable Team utilizing Helicath and Maneater to stay unkillable and affinity friendly.

1 Key UNM is possible with very good gear and meta champions.

## Slot Rules

### Slot 2 · Seeker

If Seeker takes the first stun, simply use his a1 on his next available attack (turning a 3 turn delay into a 5 turn delay on his a2)

## Notes

Unkillable Team utilizing a 4 Turn UK Champ and Maneater to stay unkillable and affinity friendly.



ALL SPEEDS LISTED ARE TRUE SPEEDS.
Speeds listed exclude any speed aura.  Speed Auras can be used to lower the BUILD SPEEDS.
Use the calculator to check speeds and take into account any Area Bonuses your account may have.
Make sure to prioritize all the skills in the Team Setup.

Speed Ranges:


As with nearly every Speed Tune, there are more speeds that will work than are listed, but these are the ones tested that work best and most consistently for the approved difficulties and affinities.  Generally changing the speeds of one champion can change the speeds needed of other champions.  Feel free to play around in the calculator to find what works best for your team.  There are wider ranges and alternative speeds for just one difficulty. 





255 – ManEater
218 – Seeker
214-217 – DPS
192-199 – 4 turn Cooldown UK Champ
179-183 – DPS



Speed Tune Sync:



UNM tune will sync on turn 6
NM tune will sync on turn 9
Brutal tune will sync on turn 3

Skill Set Up:



UNM

ManEater: Open with A1, then A3
Seeker: Open with A1, then A1, then A1, then A2

3 Turn Delay on A2
5 Turn Delay on A2 if Seeker is going to take the 1st Stun


4 Turn UK Champ: Open with A1, then A1, then A1, then Unkillable Skill


NM

ManEater: Open with A1, then A3
Seeker: Open with A1, then A1, then A1, then A1, then A1, then A2

5 Turn Delay on A2


4 Turn UK Champ: Open with A1, then A1, then A1, then Unkillable Skill


Brutal

ManEater: Open with A1, then A1, then A3
Seeker: Open with A1, then A1, then A1, then A1, then A2

4 Turn Delay on A2


4 Turn UK Champ: Open with Unkillable Skill



Gear, Masteries, Blessings:



Gear

Any sets that don’t affect turn meter or skill timing for the key players (ManEater/Seeker/4 Turn UK).  No relentless.


Masteries

Stick to typical Masteries that won’t affect Turn Meter or skill timing.


Blessings

Stick to typical Clan Boss Blessings.  ( 1 Brimstone, 1 Cruelty, Remainder Phantom Touch )



Advanced Notes:



You’ll want to avoid the stun hitting Maneater or 4 Turn Unkillable Champ on the first stun.  Place them last in the turn order and ensure you have someone of weak and/or neutral affinity ahead of them in the team order.

DISCLAIMER: Helicath’s passive places Increase DEF and after further testing, on void affinity it’s causing the stun to goto Maneater for the first 2 stun hits. So a work around this is a 7 turn delay on Helicath’s A3 (A2, A1, A1, A2, A1, A1, A2 then you can auto)
