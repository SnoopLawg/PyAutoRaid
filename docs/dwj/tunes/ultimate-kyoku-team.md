# Ultimate Kyoku Team

**Type**: Traditional · **Difficulty**: Hard · **Key**: 1 Key UNM · **Affinity**: All Affinities · **Author**: DeadwoodJedi

Source: https://deadwoodjedi.com/speed-tunes/ultimate-kyoku-team/

Affinity Friendly 2:1 team using Kyoku with Krisk and a Buff Extender to dominate the Clan Boss.

## Slot Rules

### Slot 1 · Debuff Blocker

You can use a 4 turn debuff blocker like Sepulcher Sentinel here as well, just give her skill a 1 turn delay on UNM.

## Notes

Using the extend buff ability of both Krisk and an Extender like Godseeker Aniri or Sandlashed Survivor, we’re able to keep the buffs up in an endless cycle!

Speed Ranges:


As with nearly every Speed Tune, there are more speeds that will work than are listed, but these are the ones tested that work best and most consistently for the approved difficulties and affinities.  Generally changing the speeds of one champion can change the speeds needed of other champions.  Feel free to play around in the calculator to find what works best for your team.  There are wider ranges and alternative speeds for just one difficulty.

Skill Set Up:



UNM / NM

Block Debuff: Open A1, then Block Debuff Skill
Krisk: Open A1
Buff Extender: Open Buff Extension Skill
Kyoku: Open A1
This is a full auto setup when skills are pre-set.



Gear, Masteries, Blessings:



Gear

Any sets that don’t affect turn meter or skill timing for the key players (Debuff Blocker/Buff Extender/Krisk/Kyoku).
No relentless.


Masteries

Stick to typical Masteries that won’t affect Turn Meter or skill timing.


Blessings

Stick to typical Clan Boss Blessings.  ( 1 Brimstone, 1 Cruelty, Remainder Phantom Touch )



Advanced Notes:



Ranges wider than those listed will work though they will alter the Turn Order of the champions.
