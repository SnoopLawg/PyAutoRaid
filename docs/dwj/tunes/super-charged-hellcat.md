# Super Charged Hellcat

**Type**: Unkillable · **Difficulty**: Moderate · **Key**: 2 Key UNM · **Affinity**: All Affinities · **Author**: DeadwoodJedi

Source: https://deadwoodjedi.com/speed-tunes/super-charged-hellcat/

Unkillable team using Helicath to block damage on the clan boss AOE hits and his Shield to prevent dying from the stun.  This version has TWO 4:3 ratio champion to provide more damage.

1 Key UNM is possible with very good gear and meta champions.

## Slot Rules

### Slot 2 · DPS 4:3

This champion will not be protected for the stun, so avoid.

### Slot 3 · DPS 4:3

If using a 4 turn Block Debuff Champion (like Sepulcher Sentinel) they would go here. Adding a buff extender to the 171 slot will make this fully affinity friendly.

### Slot 4 · DPS

Speeds for Nightmare are 172-178

### Slot 5 · DPS

If using a 3 turn Debuff Blocker use this slot.

## Notes

There are generally two variations to this comp, one fast and one slow. Both are listed below.  The slow is recommended generally as it’s usable on Nightmare as well as Ultra-Nightmare, with some minor gear adjustments.


Using the original Tower/Skullcrusher Unkillable, but utilizing Helicath to block damage on the clan boss AOE hits and his Shield to prevent dying from the stun. Allows a cleanser or debuff blocker keep your team stun friendly against affinity CB.

Advanced Notes:



You’ll want to avoid the stun going to anyone who will drop the shield from Helicath.  Generally this will mean avoiding your 221 speed champion.
You will need a block debuff champion with a 3 or 4 turn cooldown on that skill.  With a 3 turn cooldown a 2 turn duration is required. With a 4 turn cooldown, you’ll need a 2 turn duration as well as a buff extender (in the 171 spot). In all cases, unless tanking the stun, you’ll want to avoid the stun hitting your 221 speed 4:3 Champion, Helicath and your slowest Champion.
