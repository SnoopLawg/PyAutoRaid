# Budget Maneater Unkillable – Ninja

**Type**: Unkillable · **Difficulty**: Moderate · **Key**: 2 Key UNM · **Affinity**: All Affinities · **Author**: DeadwoodJedi

Source: https://deadwoodjedi.com/speed-tunes/budget-maneater-unkillable-ninja/

A variation of the popular Budget Unkillable that uses a very annoying champ to tune, Ninja! A great option to increase your Damage per Key.

## Notes

3in1 Budget UK w/ Ninja



ALL SPEEDS LISTED ARE TRUE SPEEDS.
Speeds listed exclude any speed aura.  Speed Auras can be used to lower the BUILD SPEEDS.
Use the calculator to check speeds and take into account any Area Bonuses your account may have.
Make sure to prioritize all the skills in the Team Setup.

Speed Ranges:


As with nearly every Speed Tune, there are more speeds that will work than are listed, but these are the ones tested that work best and most consistently for the approved difficulties and affinities.  Generally changing the speeds of one champion can change the speeds needed of other champions.  Feel free to play around in the calculator to find what works best for your team.  There are wider ranges and alternative speeds for just one difficulty.

Speed Tune Sync:



UNM tune will sync on turn 3
NM tune will sync on turn 6

Skill Set Up:



UNM

ManEater: Open A3
Painkeeper: Open A3, then A2

For Spirit Affinity wait to use Painkeeper’s A2 until Turn 5


Ninja: Open A3, then A2
This is a full auto setup when skills are pre-set.


NM

ManEater: Open A2, then A3
Painkeeper: Open A3, then A1, then A2
Ninja: Open A3, then A2


Brutal

ManEater: Open A2, then A1, then A3
Painkeeper: Open A2, then A1, then A3
Ninja: Open A2, then A3



Gear, Masteries, Blessings:



Gear

Any sets that don’t affect turn meter or skill timing for the key players (ManEater/Painkeeper/Ninja).  No relentless.
No Counter Attack Gear or Accessories for Ninja or Painkeeper


Masteries

Stick to typical Masteries that won’t affect Turn Meter or skill timing.
No Counter Attack Masteries for Ninja or Painkeeper


Blessings

Stick to typical Clan Boss Blessings.  ( 1 Brimstone, 1 Cruelty, Remainder Phantom Touch )



Advanced Notes:



Ninja

A3 must be fully booked.
A3 must be prioritized as 1st choice in AI setup.
A2 must be prioritized as 2nd choice in AI setup.


Force Affinity Clan Boss

Swap Ninja out for a normal DPS ( 175 – 178 ) and follow Standard Setup.
