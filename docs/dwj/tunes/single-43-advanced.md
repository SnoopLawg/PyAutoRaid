## Slot Rules

### Slot 1 · 4:3 Champion

Speed of the 3rd fastest champion will determine where their double turn occurs.

### Slot 2 · Counter-Attacker / Block Debuff Champion / DPS

If less than 20 speed faster than slowest DPS (i.e. 191) will be perfect for Counter-Attacker by going last in the turn order.  Will NOT go last on the turn where the 4:3 champion takes a double turn.

Can be useful for either a Counter-Attack Champion (if going last before AOE’s) or Debuff Blocker on a 3 turn CD (if going last between AOE’s).

### Slot 3 · DPS

Speed will determine where their double turn occurs.  Generally from 172-177 and 178-185 offer different rotations.

### Slot 5 · Counter-Attacker / DPS

At 171 speed will go last in the turn order ALWAYS, perfect for a Counter-Attack champion.

Speed Tune Sync:



Timing of the double turn is determined by the speed of the 3rd fastest champion, the turn order is determined by the speed of the 2nd fastest champion:

 UNM

If the second fastest champion (191-198) is less than 20 speed faster than the slowest champion, they will go last in turn order, but the 4:3 champion will go first AND last when taking their double turn. This is good for both Counter-attack and Block debuff champions.
If the second fastest champion (191-198) is 20 speed faster or more than the slowest champion, then the slowest champion will go last in turn order always. This is good for Counter-Attack Champions.
If 3rd fastest champion (172-185) is 177 speed or less, the 4:3 champion takes their double turn after Stun.
If 3rd fastest champion (172-185) is 178 speed or more, the 4:3 champion takes their double turn after after AOE 1.


NM




239-244 champ will move on a 3:2 ratio, taking a double turn every other clan boss attack, going last on each double turn.  If the stun target and is the block debuff champion (2 turn duration, 4 turn cooldown) will still stay in sync due to the delayed turn.





Gear, Masteries, Blessings:



Gear

Any sets that don’t affect turn meter or skill timing for the key players.
No relentless.


Masteries

Stick to typical Masteries that won’t affect Turn Meter or skill timing.


Blessings

Stick to typical Clan Boss Blessings.  ( 1 Brimstone, 1 Cruelty, Remainder Phantom Touch )



Advanced Notes:



This speed tune is useful for having one champ on a 4-turn cooldown using their abilities every cycle.
Typical build used to maximize Sepulcher Sentinel or Duchess’ block debuff skill by timing their double turn to occur between the AOE’s and thus block the affinity debuffs AND the stun each cycle.
Can alternatively be used to add an extra turn to accomodate the stun debuff, typically with a champion like Martyr you want to go last in the turn order before the AOE’s occur.
