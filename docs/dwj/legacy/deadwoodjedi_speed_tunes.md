4. **Masteries:** Avoid anything that affects Turn Meter or skill timing (e.g., Arcane Celerity, Cycle of Revenge)
5. **Preferred Mastery:** Warmaster for all positions
6. **Blessings:** 1 Brimstone, 1 Cruelty, Remainder Phantom Touch
7. **Use the DWJ Calculator** to verify -- account for speed sets and Lore of Steel mastery
8. **Speed auras** can be used to lower build speeds -- use calculator to adjust
9. **Skill priorities matter** -- set them in AI presets before going full auto
10. **Cooldown delays** must be set correctly in the calculator for the tune to sync
